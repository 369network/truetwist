## Community Engagement Protocol

### Comment Response Rules
- **Response time:** < 15 minutes during launch day (6 AM - 11 PM PT)
- **Tone:** Friendly, founder-authentic, grateful, technically knowledgeable
- **Negative feedback:** Acknowledge, explain roadmap, never get defensive
- **Feature requests:** "Great idea! Adding to our roadmap." (if reasonable)
- **Competitor comparisons:** Honest, highlight TrueTwist's unique value, never trash-talk

### Templated Responses

**For "How is this different from [Buffer/Hootsuite/Later]?"**
> Great question! Tools like Buffer and Hootsuite are scheduling tools — they help you post content you've already created. TrueTwist actually creates the content for you using AI that understands your brand voice, then researches viral trends in your niche, and posts it automatically. It's the difference between a scheduling assistant and a full social media team.

**For "Does it really work with AI-generated content?"**
> Yes! And we've been deliberate about quality. Our AI doesn't just generate generic posts — it learns your brand voice, studies your competitors, and incorporates real-time trends. Our beta users saw 180% higher engagement compared to their manually-created content. The key is that AI handles the repetitive work while maintaining your unique voice.

**For "What about [specific platform] support?"**
> We currently support Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube, and Pinterest. Each platform gets content specifically optimized for its format, algorithm, and audience. Not just cross-posting — true platform-native content.

**For pricing objections:**
> We have a free tier that lets you try TrueTwist with 2 platforms and 10 posts/month — no credit card required. Most users upgrade to Pro ($79/mo) once they see the time savings. Plus, PH users get 50% off for 3 months!
