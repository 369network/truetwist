04 — Motion Tokens

Motion Design Token System

Standardized motion primitives used consistently across all video templates to maintain a cohesive, professional animation language.

⏱
Duration Tokens
TokenValueVisual
--dur-instant 100ms
--dur-fast 200ms
--dur-normal 300ms
--dur-slow 600ms
--dur-dramatic 1200ms
⚡
Easing Tokens
TokenCubic-Bezier
--ease-default cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
--ease-spring cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)
--ease-bounce cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)
--ease-dramatic cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
--ease-smooth cubic-bezier(0.45, 0, 0.55, 1)
Stagger Pattern: delay = base-delay × index
Example: base-delay: 150ms, 4 items → [0ms, 150ms, 300ms, 450ms]
